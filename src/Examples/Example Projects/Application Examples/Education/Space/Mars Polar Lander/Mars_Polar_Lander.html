MARS POLAR LANDER
Launch date: January 3, 1999

Description: This example shows the Mars Polar Lander after touchdown.

The Mars Polar Lander was designed to search for evidence of past or present life, understand the climate and volatile history, and gather an inventory of the possible resources on Mars. The Lander was lost in December of 1999.

Controls: Click and drag on the image to change the viewpoint.

Keyboard controls:
C - Close the Solar Panels
O - Open the Solar Panels
U - Raise the camera
D - Lower the camera
R - Rotate the camera counterclockwise
E - Rotate the camera clockwise
= - Press and hold to start thrusters
- - Press and hold to cut thrusters

If you do not see animation in the content, drag your mouse over the window.
