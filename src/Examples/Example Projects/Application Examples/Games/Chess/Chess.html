Virtual Chess!

Controls:

To play, simply left-click on a piece and drag to move it. A "virtual" wireframe piece will indicate where you're moving the piece. If the virtual piece is red, it is an invalid move; if the virtual piece is white, it is a valid move. Remember that the white team moves first!

To rotate the board, left-click away from a piece and drag.

To move the board closer or further away, press both mouse buttons (or Shift-Option-click) and drag up or down.
