Score points by destroying marbles. Click once to select a group of two or more marbles of the same color and to see the possible score for that group. Click the group again to destroy those marbles and score their points. Click elsewhere to deselect the group. After marbles are destroyed, marbles above will fall down and empty columns will be filled. Larger groups of marbles are worth more points.

The game ends when the board is empty, or there are no groups of two or more marbles.

If the game seems slow, press 'x' to simplify the display.
KeyAction
- Make the graphics simpler, improving the speed of the game
+ Make the graphics more complex, improving the appearance of the game
t Toggle texturing of the marbles. Not using textures may speed up the game
r Toggle rotating the marbles. Not rotating the marbles may speed up the game
x Activate "fast mode". In fast mode texturing of marbles and rotating marbles is disabled, and the marbles are drawn in low detail.
z Switch between cubes and marbles
w Wireframe mode
s Shaded mode
p Point plot mode
h Hidden line mode
a In wireframe mode, show all edges
o In wireframe mode, show outline edges
l In wireframe mode, show silhouette edges
f In shaded mode, show face shading
v In shaded mode, show smooth shading
